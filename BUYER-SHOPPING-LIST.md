## Notes for Buyer

- **Sample first:** Before buying 4 of each, order 1x webcam, 1x capture card, 1x Tuya sync box. These items are commonly mislabelled. We need to test them before committing.
- **TVs — verify in store:** Turn on the TV and look in Settings. It must say "Google TV" or "Android TV" as the operating system. If you see "Roku", "Vidaa", "Tizen", or "webOS" — it's the wrong one.
- **UPS — "pure sine wave" is critical.** The words "pure sine wave" must appear on the box or spec sheet. Cheaper UPS units will damage the computers.
- **Capture card chipset:** The listing must mention "MS2130". If it says "MS2109" or doesn't mention a chipset — it's the wrong one.
- **Both PCs must be identical.** Same model, same generation, same specs. This is for emergency backup — one replaces the other.
- **Keep all receipts and boxes** for at least 30 days in case items need to be returned or exchanged.
